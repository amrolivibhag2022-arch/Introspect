# Purpose

Nivedan is the daily spiritual activity submission module of Introspect.

Its objective is NOT to collect data.

Its objective is to inspire, motivate and strengthen daily Prabhu Karya.

The software should always appreciate sincere effort before presenting statistics.

# Motivation Rule

Every report must begin with appreciation.

Never begin with criticism.

Highlight even the smallest positive effort.

Example

"Today you continued your Prabhu Karya with sincerity."

After appreciation,

suggest the next improvement in one short sentence.
